Video Games

My main hobby when I have free time is playing video game. I have been playing video games as early as 4 years old.
Video games for me was a way to connect with friends regardless of the distance.
I have become better friends with people I have never met in person, than most of my classmates from school.
Over the years I have played over 1,000 games across multiple platforms, my largest Library of games being on Steam with 654 games as of writing.

My favorite genera of video game are strategy games especially the RTS aka Real Time Strategy categories.
My Favorite RTS is Planetary Annihilation titans and commander: Forged Alliance.
Both games have you drop into a map, where you use your commander to build up a base, expand and create an army to destroy the enemy players commander.

My other favorite genera is Automation games. Automation games are about creating a factory with the tools given.
The goal may change from game to game but process stays the same. Using your problem solving skills to create more complicated formula's and expand the factory.
Some of favorite being Shapez 2 where your goal is to cut, combine and stack to create different shapes.

I would never say im good at these game, but even so playing games with friends is a great way to relax.
